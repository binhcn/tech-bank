
implication: hệ quả, tác động
The new policy has serious implication for the economy


ever: đã từng, hiện tại hoặc tương lai vẫn còn
if you've ever worked on a large Java project in an enterprise



leverage: tận dụng
they are leveraging their brand and reputation



verbose: dài dòng, rườm rà
his verbose explanation confused the students


by that, I mean: bằng cách đó, ý tôi là


derive: suy ra
we derived this formula from the basic principles


upon: ngay khi, ngay lúc
upon arriving, she called me



prescribe: quy định, chỉ định
the law prescribes that all citizens must register

enforce: thi hành, thực thi quy tắc
the police officer was called to enfore the traffic laws

spell out: giải thích rõ ràng, chi tiết
the teacher had to spell out the rules for the new students


one-off = one-time: chỉ xảy ra một lần, không lặp lại
The artist created an one-off painting

simple >> simplistic
he has a simplistic view of the problem


literally: theo nghĩa đen, chính xác, thực sự
this is a literally true story

as long as: miễn là, với điều kiện
You can stay here as long as you keep the room clean

materialize: vật chất hóa, cụ thể hóa
they materialized their vision into a successful business

emulate: mô phỏng >> imitate
the new software is designed to emulate older operating systems


count as: coi là, cân nhắc
he doesn't count as a member

discount: không đếm xỉa, coi nhẹ
he could discount what I said
